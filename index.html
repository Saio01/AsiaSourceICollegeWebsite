Asia Source ICollege

A relatively new school, Asia Source is abreast with other top institutions and remarkably exceeds expectations of a new school in providing quality education relevant with the needs of the times through its top of the line facilities, highly-qualified educators, and government-approved instructional content validated by the seal of recognition and certification by both the Department of Education (DepEd) and Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA). A second branch was opened at Kapasigan, Pasig City on January 23, 2018 offering the same courses we have in Taguig City.

PROGRAMS

ASiC is offering Senior High School which aims at continuously providing high quality education to become global leaders. Our SHS students will be trained by expert faculty, industry practitioners and nationally certified instructors. Our SHS curricula are focused on relevant industry courses to ensure employability, better career options and valued credentials for student’s future opportunities and lifelong learning experiences. Our SHS programs will utilize varied delivery options such ASiC Senior High School courses will be the model for student’s lifelong access opportunities in pursuing higher education and/or further training. This includes a variety of academic options and industry work-experiences and credit transfer agreements.

STEM

This strand prepares the student to take up course in Nursing, Pharmacy, Medical Sciences, Natural Sciences, Computer Science, Architecture, Engineering, etc.

ABM

This strand will allow the student to take up course in college like Business Administration, Tourism, Accountancy, Business Entrepreneurship , etc.

HUMSS

This strand prepares the student to take up college course in Journalism, Education, Philosophy, Social Sciences and Communication Arts.

GAS

This strand is for students who are not yet decided what course to take up in college. The student might be given a bridging program to assess his/her preparedness to the course he/she will choose in college

ICT

The Specialization in Computer Systems Servicing (CSS) equips individuals with skills to install, configure, and maintain computer systems and networks. The Specialization in Computer Programming (CP) focuses on developing proficiency in programming, database systems, web design, operating systems, and software applications.

HE

The Specialization in Hotel Operations (HO) enhances students' skills in housekeeping, food and beverage services, managing reservations, check-ins, and check-outs, and assisting tourists from arrival to departure in various hospitality settings.

EVENTS

Discover a vibrant calendar of educational, cultural, and community-driven events right here at Asia Source iCollege. From workshops and seminars to student showcases and fun events, our events foster collaboration, learning, and networking.

Strandfest

The annual Asia Source iCollege Strandfest brings together students from Taguig and Pasig campuses in a spirited celebration of talent, camaraderie, and healthy competition. This vibrant event features exhilarating sports competitions, where athletes showcase their skills in various disciplines. From basketball and volleyball to track and field, the Strandfest ignites the competitive spirit within our student body.

But it’s not just about sports—our Strandfest also dazzles with colorful pageants. Participants from both campuses grace the stage, exuding confidence and grace. Whether vying for the title of Mr. or Ms. Asia Source, they captivate the audience with their poise, wit, and charisma. Join us as we unite the Asia Source iCollege community, fostering friendships, teamwork, and pride. Strandfest is more than an event; it’s a testament to our shared passion for excellence and the vibrant spirit that defines our school

Acquaintance Party

Get ready to groove to the rhythm of Seoul! Our ever-evolving Acquaintance Party with the theme of the year, K-POP!, at Asia Source iCollege is a dazzling fusion of music, fashion, and culture. As the sun sets, students transform into their favorite K-Pop idols, donning vibrant outfits inspired by iconic groups and solo artists. From sleek streetwear to glittering stage costumes, the campus becomes a runway for creativity and self-expression.

The beat drops, and dance battles erupt—students showcase their best moves, mirroring choreography from BTS, BLACKPINK, EXO, and more. The air buzzes with excitement as fans cheer for their bias, and friendships form over shared love for Korean entertainment. Whether you’re a seasoned K-Pop aficionado or a curious newcomer, our Acquaintance Party celebrates the global phenomenon that transcends borders. So grab your light sticks, strike a pose, and let the Hallyu wave sweep you away!

Foundation Day

On our annual Foundation Day, Asia Source iCollege comes alive with joy, nostalgia, and a sense of purpose. It’s the day we honor our institution’s beginnings—the seeds planted by visionaries, educators, and dreamers. As the sun rises, students, faculty, and alumni gather to celebrate our shared journey.

The campus buzzes with activity: colorful banners flutter, music fills the air, and laughter echoes across the courtyard. From inspiring keynote speeches to fun-filled games, Foundation Day bridges the past and the present. We reflect on milestones, express gratitude for the community that sustains us, and look ahead to the future. Whether you’re a freshman or a seasoned graduate, Foundation Day reminds us that we’re part of something bigger—a legacy that continues to shape lives and empower dreams. So raise your cupcake, wear your school colors, and let’s toast to the spirit of Asia Source iCollege!

APSA-TAPAT

A spectacular display of unity and passion! The APSA-TAPAT Opening Ceremony was a vibrant celebration as 19 schools came together, proudly marching with their school flags and sporting their athletic uniforms. Asia Source iCollege added to the excitement by presenting a captivating Muse and escort, symbolizing the spirit of our institution.

This event reminds us of the importance of a balanced high school journey, where students not only excel academically but also embrace a fun and enjoyable life. Let the games begin as we embark on a remarkable season of growth, camaraderie, and unforgettable experiences!

Year-End Party

As the academic year gracefully concludes, Asia Source iCollege invites you to our spirited Year-End Celebration. This isn’t your typical formal affair—instead, picture a lively gathering where students, faculty, and staff come together. Whether you’re in jeans, sneakers, or your favorite hoodie, you’re part of the festivities.

The beat of the music sets the tone as laughter fills the room. We honor achievements, share stories, and express gratitude for the moments that shaped us. As the clock ticks toward midnight, we raise our metaphorical glasses—to friendship, resilience, and the promise of new beginnings. So let loose, dance like nobody’s watching, and let the memories of this year linger in our hearts. Cheers to Asia Source iCollege—the place where learning meets laughter, and where every day is worth celebrating!

FACILITIES

Asia Source iCollege features state-of-the-art facilities, including modern computer labs in the ICT faculty, equipped with the latest technology for hands-on learning in computer systems and programming. The HO faculty offers simulated hotel environments with housekeeping labs, food and beverage service areas, and front office training rooms for practical hospitality management experience. Additionally, the school provides spacious, well-equipped classrooms that foster an engaging learning atmosphere across all disciplines, ensuring students receive top-quality education and training for their future careers.
